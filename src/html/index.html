One Day, One Track, One Community.

ServerlessDays are a family of events around the world focused on fostering a community around serverless technologies.

Each ServerlessDay is unique, organised by local organisors and not run for profit. They are run according the following principles.

  • Run by local community organisors
  • Financially and physically accessible to the community
  • Be representative of the broader community within which it exists beyond technology
